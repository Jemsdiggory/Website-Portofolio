About Me

My name is Kahlaa Aulia Jemima, I'm a Game Technology student at Politeknik Negeri Media Kreatif Jakarta, currently focused on building my skills in game programming, especially using Unity.

Most of the games I upload in itch.io are team projects where I mainly take the role of programmer. I enjoy experimenting with mechanics, gameplay systems, and sometimes help out with video editing or design too. Everything you see here is part of my learning journey. I hope you enjoy playing these games as much as I enjoyed creating (and debugging!) them.

Got feedback, ideas, or want to collaborate? Feel free to reach out, I'm always up for chatting about game development!
